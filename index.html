Biography

Shuangming Chai is currently working at Shining 3D as an algorithm engineer. He received a PhD degree in Computational Mathematics in 2019 from University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Ligang Liu and Dr. Xiao-Ming Fu. He received a BSc degree in 2014 from University of Science and Technology of China. He worked in Microsoft Research Asia as an intern from July 2013 to May 2014, mentored by Dr. Yang Liu.

Research Interests

Major in computer graphics, including modeling, geometric processing, 3D printing, etc.
