Biography

Shuangming Chai is currently a post-doctor at University of Science and Technology of China. He was an algorithm engineer at Shining 3D Tech Co., Ltd. from July 2019 to November 2021. He received a PhD degree in Computational Mathematics in 2019 from University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Ligang Liu and Prof. Xiao-Ming Fu. He received a BSc degree in Information and Computing Science in 2014 from University of Science and Technology of China. He worked at Microsoft Research Asia as an intern from July 2013 to May 2014, mentored by Dr. Yang Liu.

Research Interests

Major in computer graphics, including modeling, geometric processing, 3D printing, etc.
